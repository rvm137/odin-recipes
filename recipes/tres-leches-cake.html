Tres Leches Cake

Tres Leches is a wonderful cake from Mexican culture that involves the use of three different types of milk; hence the name: Tres Leches. Soaked in milk this cake still holds a spongu and fluffy texture that feels like you are eating fresh cake that you just dipped in milk.

Ingredients Needed To Make The Cake:

  • 1 1/2 cups all-pupose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups of whole milk
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can of evaporated milk

Ingredients For The Topping:

  • 1 1/2 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps to making this fantastic dish:

  1. Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Grease and flour a 9x13-inch baking pan.
  2. Steo 2: Sift flour and baking powder together; set aside.
  3. Step 3: Beat sugar and butter together in a large bowl with an electric mixer until light and fluffy.
  4. Step 4: Add eggs and vanilla to the bowl, enure you mix well. Then add flour mixture, about 1/2 cup at a time, mixing until well blended and pour the batter into the prepared pan once done mixing.
  5. Step 5: Throw it in the preheated oven for around 30 mins. To ensure fully cooked, stick a toothpick in the center, if the the toothpick comes out clean the cake is done.
  6. Step 6: Grab a fork and poke holes all over the cake, after you are finished let it cool down to room temperature.
  7. Step 7: Grab a bowl and mix the whole mile, condensed milk, and evaporated milk together. Once mixed, pour it all over the cooled down cake and let it soak in.
  8. Step 8: In a mixer or bowl, put the heavy whipping cream, sugar, and vanilla extract in and mix thouroughly. Apply the top of the cake and keep refrigerated until it is time to enjoy.
